LDS Talk Tips Site Plan

Brynlee Bingham

WDD 131-1

Overview

Purpose

For my personal website, I wanted to create a site that would help members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints as they prepare to give talks in church. It can always be really nerve wracking to prepare and share a talk in front of a large number of people and I wanted to help relieve some of that stress. The purpose of my site is to help individuals have access to different quotes that pertain to certain gospel principles. For example, if I was writing a talk about "faith", I could search this word and quotes that were related to this topic would appear and I could incorperate them into my talk. It will be a site for talk tips! :)

Audience

The general audiance for my wedsite will most likely be those from the ages of 18 - 65. Individuals in this age range will most likely want a little extra help and resources to find quotes that will help them write a talk. Mostly members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints will use this website but all followers of Christ are welcome to read and learn from the quotes that will be found on the website. Anyone who is looking for uplifting quotes for any reason, including preparing talks are welcome to visit my site.

Dynamic elements

For my website, I want to make sure to use Javascript to make it dynamic and fun! I am going to be including a dropdown menu that will filter the content on the page. I will also be using a search bar that is similar to our recipe project but it will be for key words when it come to quotes. I will use the Document Object Model throughout my website. I will also use the array method for all of my quotes. I will use event handling for things such as clicks and search submissions. I will probably also use conditionals so that different content can be shown based on the results of a search. These are just a couple of things that I want to use in my page to make it interactive and dynamic by using Javascript.
